6.1.1 Motivation.Our preliminary study (RQ0) revealed that existing blame-based approaches face
fundamental limitations: a significant portion of BICs require traversing commit history beyond
direct blame results, and many cases involve multiple candidates requiring intelligent selection.
Current state-of-the-art approaches like LLM4SZZ [ 36] expand context around modified lines
for blame candidate collection (Section 5.1) but do not traverse commit history to systematically
explore ancestor commits.
