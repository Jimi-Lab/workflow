5.2 Evaluation Metrics
Following LLM4SZZ, we adopt standard information retrieval metrics. Let 𝑃 denote the set of
predicted BICs and 𝐺 denote the set of ground truth BICs for a given BFC.Precisionmeasures
the fraction of predictions that match ground truth BICs,Recallmeasures the fraction of ground
truth BICs that are correctly identified, andF1-scoreis the harmonic mean of Precision and Recall,
providing a balanced measure:
