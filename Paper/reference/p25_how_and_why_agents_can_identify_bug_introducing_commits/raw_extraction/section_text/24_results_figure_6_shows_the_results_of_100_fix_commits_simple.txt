4.3.4 RQ4c: Better Models and Agents.We investigate whether
Simple-SZZ-Agent’s performance generalizes across different LLM
backbones and agent frameworks, and whether cheaper or more
capable configurations can improve results.
Setup.We evaluate Simple-SZZ-Agent with two agent frameworks—
Claude Code and OpenHands—across multiple LLM backbones:
Claude Haiku 4.5, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Claude Opus 4.5, as well
as minimax-m2.5 and glm-5 for OpenHands. All experiments use
DS_LINUX-26 (100 samples).
