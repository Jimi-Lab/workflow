3 Part 1: SZZ-Agent
To test whether the capabilities of LLM-based agents translate into
effective bug-introducing commit identification, we design SZZ-
Agent, an agentic approach to identify bug-introducing commits.
In this section we explain how SZZ-Agent works (§3.1) and present
our evaluation (§3.2–§3.3).
