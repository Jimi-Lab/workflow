Transferable Writing Patterns

Motivation Pattern:
- Start from ecosystem scale and pace of change.
- Show why existing labels/taxonomies are not enough.
- Move from "what exists" to "what must be represented to support reasoning."

Problem Pattern:
- Define the domain object precisely: countermeasure, digital object, digital artifact, tactic, technique.
- Explain why semantic precision matters operationally.
- Ground abstract modeling choices in concrete examples.

Method Pattern:
- Separate framework, knowledge model, and knowledge graph.
- Use bottom-up evidence curation, then organize emergent semantic patterns.
- Use a worked example technique to explain why terminology and relation design matter.

Roadmap Pattern:
- State that the first version is manually curated and preliminary.
- Present automation as a future sustainability requirement, not as an unsupported current result.

Useful for VulnVersion:
- Use the same distinction between evidence collection, semantic modeling, graph construction, and inference.
- Make the graph serve a concrete reasoning target, not just a visualization.
