- Such provenance graphs rep- resent relationships between the control ﬂow and data ﬂow be- tween subjects (e.g., processes, threads) and objects (e.g., ﬁles, network sockets) in the system through a timestamped directed graph.
- Based on that, a large variety of techniques have been de- veloped to automatically detect and connect attack steps in such graphs.
