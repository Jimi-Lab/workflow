Experiment Design Analysis

Paper evaluation style:
- Large-scale descriptive graph statistics: entity counts, relationship counts, and coverage across CVE, CWE, CAPEC, and ATT&CK.
- Traversal case studies: forward traversal from a CVE to ATT&CK and reverse traversal from a technique/group to CVEs.
- Practical CTI workflows: top software CWE analysis, group-to-weakness/vulnerability mapping, and CISA-advisory-style investigations.
- Link discovery/hierarchical expansion: demonstrates that bounded expansions can recover relevant CVEs beyond direct mappings.

Transferable experiment pattern for VulnVersion:
- Report both graph-scale statistics and task-quality metrics. VulnVersion should report number of CVEs, repositories, versions/tags, commits, functions/files, graph edges, and affected-version accuracy metrics.
- Include bidirectional case studies: from CVE+patch to affected versions, and from a target version decision back to evidence paths.
- Evaluate direct-only traversal versus hierarchy/branch/backport expansion to show when multi-hop graph reasoning helps.
